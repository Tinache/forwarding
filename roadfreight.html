Roadfreight

Roadfreight is the physical process of transporting cargo by road using motor vehicles.

In this case, road is a lane/route between the point of departure and the point of destination.

Roadfreight can be in certain cases, the only way of transport in/to rural areas where other mode of transports is not available.
